2017 Southest Asian Games


The 2017 Southeast Asian Games(Malay:Sukan Asia Tenggara), oficially known as the 29th South East Asian Games (Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara ke-29) and commonly known as Kuala Lumpur 2017 is aregional multi-sport event that will take place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia from 19 to 31 August 2017, with 405 events in 38 sports to be featured in the games

It will be the sixt time that the South east Asian Games held in Malaysia, after 1965, 1977, 1989 and 2001 Games and its first since 2001

By the time the country host the games in 2017, it will be 16 years after Malaysia last hosted the biennial event. The games closing ceremony will coincide with Malaysia's 60th Indipendance Day.
